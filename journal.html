How to log your symptoms

Bloating (1–10) — log 2h after lunch

1–2Completely flat, no sensation at all
3–4Mild fullness, clothes comfortable, no visible change
5–6Noticeable pressure, waistband tighter, slightly visible
7–8Visibly distended, uncomfortable to bend, need to loosen clothes
9–10Severely swollen, painful to touch, can't ignore it

Pain / Cramps (1–10) — gut pain only, not hunger

1–2No pain at all
3–4Mild discomfort, aware of it but doesn't affect anything
5–6Noticeable cramping, you stop what you're doing momentarily
7–8Sharp cramps, hard to concentrate, affects your day
9–10Severe, makes you curl up, can't function normally
📍 Always note WHERE in your notes: upper abdomen, lower left, lower right, or all over.

Bathroom — be precise

✓ NormalNo straining, soft formed stool (Bristol type 3–4), felt fully empty
✓ PainfulHad to strain, over 10 min, hard/pellet stool (Bristol 1–2), felt incomplete
✗ NoZero bowel movement the entire day
PlantagoAlways log — a day with it vs without are different data points

Overall feel — whole body, not just gut

😊 GreatGood energy, no symptoms interfering, trained well, mood normal
😐 OkayMild symptoms but manageable, didn't stop you from anything
😕 OffSymptoms affecting mood, training, or concentration
😣 BadGut symptoms dominated your day, affected everything

Water — what counts

✅ CountPlain water, herbal teas, water with lemon
❌ Don'tCoffee, sparkling water (gas!), juice, water in food

When to log — same time every day

Bloating & Pain2 hours after lunch
BathroomEnd of day
Overall feelBefore bed
WaterThroughout the day
⚠️ Consistency in timing is everything. Logging at different times makes the data useless for comparison.
